date: 2024-08-25

Video games are about learning, but most games present a reality where learning is punished.
Most players enjoy games where learning is not the priority. 
Allocation of resources, numbers games, etc.

Some games are about learning a skill.
Fighting Games, MOBAs, Speedrunning games.
These are like Martial Arts.
I like these games.
They're pretty simple, if you think about it.

Most games are like movies.
People want to see a story, or watch something which gives them a nice little feeling.

I just don't really know what games are, really.
They're so many things at once.
It's almost crazy to me that you could categorize these things this way.
