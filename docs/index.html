Hi, I’m Alex.

Nice to meet you! I’m a game designer and programmer living in Bellevue, Washington.

I think a lot about games, and their potential for expression and feeling. I work on prototypes that explore these ideas. I love having control over my work. My games mostly use custom, from-scratch engines.

I like to write music and shoot films sometimes too.

No matter the medium, I like art that is simple and honest.

Thanks for stopping by. Go ahead and take a look around!
