Art-vent calendar

This month I’m going to try to make some art, because it’s fun.

I’m going to do it every day, because then I will get better quickly.

Every day I’ll post something I make here.

Since art can always be tweaked, I am setting my goal at the end of the month to be efficiency. I want to be able to create a piece of art that is of high quality in a short time (e.g. not 5 hours).
