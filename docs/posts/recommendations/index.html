Recommendations

This is a simple list of recommendations to anyone who is looking for something good to experience. My tendency is towards recommending off-beat stuff that you might not have heard of before.

Films

Wolf Children is the most personal movie I’ve ever seen. Takagi Masakatsu’s soundtrack brings me sheer joy every single time I watch it.

Angel’s Egg is harrowing. Mamoru Oshii before Ghost in the Shell.

Anime

Gunbuster was doing relativity tragedy almost thirty years before Interstellar.

The Tatami Galaxy is a joy to watch, and is the best of what animation has to offer.

Books

The War of Art is great. I reviewed it here.

A Theory of Fun is revolutionary. I reviewed it here.

The Art of Learning is packed with insights.

Music

Textbooks

Tutorials

Miziziziz has the best tutorials for game dev on the internet by far. His courses are the definition of concise, never wasting a moment of your time.
