My attention flags.
I seek inputs, but to what end?
Books are full of knowledge. Movies are full of ideas. Games are full of
intuitions.
Is that what they're really for?
To me, I mean. Do I really see books as an opportunity to glean knowledge, or to
entertain myself? To keep myself from thinking personal thoughts? To present
myself a certain way? To prove who I am to myself?
No one acts in good faith; true to the meaning of their cause.
To be aligned, that is truth.
