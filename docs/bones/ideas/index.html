Bones - A series of Role-Playing games by Alex Hartford.

Ideas for future games

This document keeps all the ideas we’ve come up with for the game.

Top Ideas

Triage

Below are all the ideas we’ve had.

Siege

You can run the people laying siege, what it’s like to be individual soldiers during a battle of this kind.

Then you can run a couple of street rats within the castle, trying to survive in this environment, maybe to sneak out and steal supplies.

Finally, when the battle erupts, you can play as the last surviving royal guards, tasked with fleeing the burning castle and taking the princess to safety.

Then you could even play as the princess, when her knights are killed by bandits, who goes into a life of skullduggery!

Survivors of a great battle

A group of survivors bands together to escape a battlefield after a bloody defeat. They will either be cut down, one by one, or find some way to band together and escape to their homes.

The Invincible Swordsman

Any story can start this one. A character beats overwhelming odds, fights a dozen men and wins, and survives to tell the tale. This character could come back later, as a special character to assist younger ones. Once a character has fulfilled their story, their life need not be over!

Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon

A thief steals the Legendary Sword from its resting place. Its master comes to retrieve it, but senses their potential, and ends up training the young thief.

Life or Death

A student of the sword believes they are ready to challenge anyone, but they have never fought a duel to the death before. Could be Wudang swordsmanship or any of the Japanese variants.

Murder story

Future: “You may become a monk, but you must vow not to commit murder”. The man does not speak. “Do you vow it?” … Then we tell the story of what the character did in the past!
