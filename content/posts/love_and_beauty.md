Rupert Spira's formulation of these things is like this:

When one realizes that they are infinite consciousness, that they are what "I Am", and that we all share this one singular conscious awareness, there are two changes to the way we see the world.

These are:
* Love, which you feel because you know you are connected to other people.
* Beauty, which you feel because you know you are connected to the world of things.

A simple, beautiful formulation.
If you're interested in more, find his podcast episode with Rupert Sheldrake.
It's a very pleasant conversation!
