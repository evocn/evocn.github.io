You ever heard of the French Republican Calendar? You should check it out!

Now don't get me wrong, I'm a huge fan of the Gregorian.
But there's some great ideas here that are fun to daydream about!

12 30-day months per year. That's pretty standard. But what about the other 5/6 days?
Just fill in the overtime with a few days at the end. That week becomes a sacred holiday!

But the weeks is really where we've got some innovation. Each month split into 3 10-day weeks.
Sounds long, but you could do all sorts of stuff with them!
You could have a day off at the beginning, middle, and end. You could do 6 days on, 4 days off. You'd obviously have to run the numbers to see how things best fit together, but 10 days instead of 7 might have some real interesting benefits.

Maybe people would start to change their thought, to be more long-term.
Plus, a week off is a really big deal when it's 10 whole days!

It's a shame things are so standardized now, since ideas like this will never really have a chance.
In any case, fun to think about!
