This is a simple list of recommendations to anyone who is looking for something
good to experience. My tendency is towards recommending off-beat stuff that you
might not have heard of before.

## Films
[**Wolf Children**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wolf_Children) is the most
personal movie I've ever seen. Takagi Masakatsu's soundtrack brings me sheer joy
every single time I watch it.

[**Angel's Egg**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angel%27s_Egg) is harrowing.
Mamoru Oshii before *Ghost in the Shell*.

## Anime
[**Gunbuster**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gunbuster) was doing relativity
tragedy almost thirty years before Interstellar.

[**The Tatami Galaxy**]() is a joy to watch, and is the best of what animation
has to offer.

## Books
[**The War of Art**](https://www.amazon.com/War-Art-Steven-Pressfield-ebook/dp/B007A4SDCG) is great. I reviewed it [here](/posts/warofart).

[**A Theory of Fun**](https://www.theoryoffun.com) is revolutionary. I reviewed it [here](/posts/theoryoffun).

[**The Art of Learning**](https://www.amazon.com/Art-Learning-Journey-Optimal-Performance/dp/0743277465) is packed with insights.
